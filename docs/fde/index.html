Why This Role Exists

A strategic bet, not an experiment.

Most public companies treat AI in Finance as a procurement exercise — license a vendor, run a pilot, produce a slide. At Omada, we are dramatically rethinking Finance — building an AI-native function from the ground up. The way to get there is to hire a builder, embed them inside the team, and hand over the roadmap.

This is the first dedicated AI engineering role inside Omada's Finance organization. You won't be a shared service, a matrixed partner, or a committee participant. You'll sit with the operators, build alongside the executives, and own what ships.

The scope is clear: 10+ production agents over 12 months, spanning financial reporting quality, operational intelligence, competitive and earnings analysis, narrative automation, and evaluation frameworks. The stakes are real — these systems will touch numbers that appear in board decks, earnings calls, and SEC filings.
